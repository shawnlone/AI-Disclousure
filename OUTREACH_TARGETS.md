## Directory Submission Copy

Short description:

```text
AI Disclosure Generator is a free browser-based tool that creates clear AI-use disclosure statements for blogs, YouTube videos, Steam/game pages, client work, education materials, marketing pages, and AI-generated images.
```

Long description:

```text
AI Disclosure Generator helps creators and indie makers explain how AI assisted their work. Choose the content type, AI tools, AI use cases, human review level, tone, and output language. The tool returns a polished disclosure statement, a short social version, structured bullet points, and clarity notes for sensitive cases. It supports English, Chinese, and bilingual output. No login, no saved input, and no AI API call.
```

## Sponsor Qualification Checklist

Pitch only if at least 3 are true:

- Their audience includes creators, indie makers, writers, marketers, educators, freelancers, or AI tool builders.
- Their product benefits from being associated with AI transparency or publishing workflows.
- They have a public website, payment page, or product page.
- They are small enough that a $10 experimental sponsor slot is plausible.
- They have posted recently, shipped recently, or are actively asking for feedback.

Skip if:

- The product encourages deception, spam, fake reviews, school cheating, scraping abuse, impersonation, or unclear payment behavior.
- The sponsor would make later ad-network approval harder.

## 30-Message Sprint

Do this in one focused session:

1. Send 10 messages to tiny AI tools.
2. Send 10 messages to creator/template sellers.
3. Send 5 messages to newsletters or curators.
4. Send 5 messages to founders who recently launched an AI workflow product.

Stop after 30, then wait 12 to 24 hours before following up.
